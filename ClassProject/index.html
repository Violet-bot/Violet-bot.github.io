News   and   Events

Information management is a social activity in which human beings plan, organize, lead and control information resources by means of modern information technology in order to effectively develop and utilize information resources. Simply put, information management is people's management of information resources and information activities. Information management refers to the whole process of management, people collect, process and input, output information. The process of information management includes information collection, information transmission, information processing and information storage.
